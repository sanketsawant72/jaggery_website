    About Us

    Rooted in Tradition,
    Committed to Purity

    Klass Exim Corporate Pvt. Ltd. — a registered manufacturer, producer and exporter of organic jaggery products operating from Navi Mumbai, Maharashtra. Serving groceries, supermarkets, retailers and exporters with fresh, chemical-free jaggery.

    Who We Are

    Klass Exim Corporate Pvt. Ltd.

    We are a topnotch business enterprise catering to demand for consumables such as manufacturing of Jaggery & Jaggery Products. Our company proudly meets requirements in a swift manner to help several groceries, supermarkets, commercial shops and others.

    We supply our offered range in bulk to suffice needs of customers in the retail industry. Our business entity has its own farm for producing certain products — we maintain a healthy farm to provide genuine quality in the market.

    Our company has entered business in Panvel, Maharashtra, India, running operations independently as a manufacturer, producer and exporter to complete needs of unique and fresh edible products. We are determined to offer not just fresh but healthy products that support overall wellness of masses.

    Our products are catered to street vendors, big retailers, small groceries and more. We maintain an easy flow in the market of our healthy and tasty edibles in order to suffice growing needs of final customers.

    Our product range encompasses Jaggery Cube, Jaggery Powder and Jaggery Syrup and several others. We protect our products from moisture, debris, mixing and other sorts of defects — solely providing fresh and healthy edibles in the food industry.
